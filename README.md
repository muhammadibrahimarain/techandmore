# Tech & More - Landing Page

Professional landing page for TechAndMore Limited, a UK-based data technology consultancy.

---

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Open the Website
1. Navigate to the `techandmore-landing` folder
2. Double-click `index.html`
3. Your browser will open the website

**OR**

Right-click `index.html` → "Open with" → Choose your browser

---

## Website Structure

```
techandmore-landing/
├── index.html              ← Homepage (start here)
├── data-solutions.html     ← Data services page
├── ai-services.html        ← AI services page
├── marketing-services.html ← Marketing services page
├── ecommerce-services.html ← E-commerce services page
├── ecommerce.html          ← E-commerce store/products
├── privacy.html            ← Privacy policy (UK GDPR)
├── terms.html              ← Terms & conditions
├── style.css               ← All styling
├── script.js               ← Interactive features
└── images/                 ← All images and GIFs
```

---

## Pages Overview

### 1. Homepage (`index.html`)
- **Hero**: "Transforming Ideas Into Reality" with animated GIF
- **Services**: 4 clickable service cards (2×2 grid)
- **About**: Company information with statistics
- **Contact**: Contact form + contact information

### 2. Service Pages
Each service page includes:
- Black background hero with white text
- Animated GIF specific to service
- 6+ detailed sub-services
- CTA section with "Request a Consultation"
- Full navigation and footer

### 3. Legal Pages
- `privacy.html` - UK GDPR compliant privacy policy
- `terms.html` - UK terms and conditions

## Navigation

### From Homepage:
- Click any service card → Go to service detail page
- Click "Tech & More" logo → Refresh/scroll to top
- Use navbar links → Navigate to sections

### From Service Pages:
- Click "Tech & More" logo → Return to homepage
- Click "View Other Services" → Go to services section
- Use navbar → Navigate anywhere
